The heart of the forge

One Codex. Every tool.

The Codex is your single source of truth — every name table, magic item, stat block, and price list lives there, and every tool reads from it live.

Edit a table once and every generator uses it immediately. Add your homebrew items and they appear in loot, in shops, in the party's inventory. No importing per tool, no copies drifting out of sync.

Your whole library exports to a single JSON file — back it up, move it, or share it with another GM in one click.
